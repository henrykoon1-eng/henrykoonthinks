# Waking in Woods: A Poem

*Originally published on [2024-06-20](https://henrykoonthinks.blogspot.com/2024/06/waking-in-woods-poem.html)*

To rise in the morning and breath cool fresh air the smell of smoke lingering on tent, shirt and hair an empty bottle of whiskey lying somewhere over there To wake to a groan in the distance of cattle or of bear These lovely mountains are for all to love and to share Come love them yourself, even stay if you dare
