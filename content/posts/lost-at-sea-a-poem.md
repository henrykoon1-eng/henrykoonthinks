# Lost at Sea: A Poem

*Originally published on [2023-12-08](https://henrykoonthinks.blogspot.com/2023/12/lost-at-sea.html)*

The sun of gold is spent and I cannot see&nbsp; Dark, cold, and violent the waters rise about me&nbsp; Under foot I've no hold, I am sent out to sea&nbsp; I grow old and shall neither repent nor see victory
