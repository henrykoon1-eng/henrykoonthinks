# The Calling: A Poem

*Originally published on [2024-01-29](https://henrykoonthinks.blogspot.com/2024/01/the-calling.html)*

&nbsp; The mountains are calling and I must go Where breeze and brooke whisper of things I ought to know Oh to be once more&nbsp; a humble&nbsp;champion upon a great peak of snow Shredded to my core as the wild winds blow I see clearly within&nbsp;myself just as the vast terrain below Striding amidst &nbsp;such trees magnificent and unknown I again will grow Forgotten is society and all of its trivial woes The mountains are calling and I must go
